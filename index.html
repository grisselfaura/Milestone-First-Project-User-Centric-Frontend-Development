About us

We are a Peruvian company committed to sustainable and responsible agriculture that seeks to obtain agricultural products to meet quality and safety standards. Agronut Peru Sac, has been working with products for foliar and soil application that satisfy the need of crops. Our offer also extends to products for the protection of crops with low environmental impact, thus promoting an integrated, responsible and environmentally friendly management. We serve nationally, including agricultural exporters and wholesalers.

Mission

To be the leading company in the field of agrochemicals in Latin America.

Vision

Satisfy the needs of the agro-industrial market, with innovative quality products.

Philosophy

Our continuous improvement ensures the profitability that allows sustainable growth.
